Mocking Louis XIV

Many of the paintings and tapestries that decorated the walls of the palace were reproduced in print so that they could be admired, studied, and copied beyond Versailles. Seen here are four such engravings after Nicolas Poussin and Charles Le Brun. Although these are “reproductive” and not “creative” works, they are not simple copies but rather individual artistic attempts to faithfully and effectively translate the original into a different medium, that of monochrome line engraving.
